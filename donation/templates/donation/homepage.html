How to Give

  • List your donation. Snap a photo. Set the drop-off time.
  • Get notified and confirm donation when you have a reservation request.
  • Get reminder when reservation time is up. And happy giving!

How to Reserve

  • View/ filter the resource map to look for things you need.
  • Click on the mark to see more detail about the donation.
  • Select pick up time and send reservation request.
  • Once confirmed by the donor, please show up on time or you may risk losing your donation.
  • That's it. Happy thrifting!
